December 2025

Guiding the Next Generation — Primary School Morning Exercise Performance

Our humanoid robot team visited the affiliated primary school to share the world of humanoid robots with younger students. During the morning assembly, the team introduced the robots, demonstrated dances, and led interactive activities. The goal was to guide the next generation into today's "AI society," helping students born after 2010 understand the trends and development of modern robotics — inspiring them to become the pillars of tomorrow's society.

January 2026

Regular Training Sessions

Consistent weekly training sessions are the backbone of PCMS-HRG's RoboCup preparation. Members work on robot programming, motion tuning, soccer strategy, and hardware maintenance — building the skills needed for international competition.

February 2026

Beijing Intensive Training Camp

In February 2026, the team traveled to Beijing for an intensive training camp, working alongside expert coaches and engineers to accelerate our technical development ahead of the upcoming RoboCup 2026 competition season. The camp covered advanced robot programming, motion optimization, and competition strategy — and also gave the team a chance to bond and grow together.

February 2026

Chinese New Year Performance

To celebrate the Chinese New Year, PCMS-HRG presented a special humanoid robot performance, bringing festive cheer and showcasing the team's progress in robot motion programming and choreography.
