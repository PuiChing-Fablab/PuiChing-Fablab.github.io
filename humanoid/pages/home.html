Welcome to PCMS-HRG

We are the Pui Ching Middle School (Macau) Humanoid Robot Group — a student team dedicated to building, programming, and competing with autonomous humanoid robots.

Our goal is to represent Macau at the RoboCup Humanoid League, one of the world's most prestigious robotics competitions. We are currently in active preparation for RoboCup, training intensively and developing our robot platform Booster T1.
