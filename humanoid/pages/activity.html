Activity

Guiding the Next Generation

2025-12-09 ~ 2025-12-13

Our school's humanoid robot team shared information about humanoid robots during the elementary school assembly, and performed exciting dances and interactive activities with humanoid robots. The goal was to guide the younger generation into today's "AI society," help them understand the trends and development of new types of robots, and strive to enable those born after 2010 to better understand the progress of today's society, so that they can also become the pillars of the future of society!
